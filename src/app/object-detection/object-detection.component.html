Object Detection


Object detection on roads is a groundbreaking technology that uses computer vision and AI to identify and classify objects like vehicles, pedestrians, and signs on roadways. By analyzing visual data from cameras and sensors, it enables safer autonomous driving, efficient traffic management, and improved road safety. This technology involves training algorithms to recognize patterns and features, allowing computers to make real-time decisions based on their understanding of the environment.

Object detection systems play a pivotal role in preventing accidents and collisions. By accurately identifying and tracking vehicles, pedestrians, and obstacles, these systems provide crucial data to autonomous vehicles and driver assistance systems, enabling them to make informed decisions and take timely actions to avoid accidents.
